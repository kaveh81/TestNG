Billboards

InstallAnywhere 8.0

Billboards are images that appear in the large right hand pane of the installer while files are being installed. Use the Billiards subtask to add billboards to the installer panels. Several billboard graphics may be added for larger (and longer) installations. For small installations, like the tutorial OfficeSuite example, only one billboard will show. Billboards may also be assigned to features, and will only be displayed if the feature they are associated with installs.

When adding multiple billboards, the billboards appear in the order they are shown in the Billboard List.

Note

The size of the billboard pane is 587x312 pixels. Installer dimensional may change slightly by platform to better display text and different fonts.
