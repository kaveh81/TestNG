Integrating FLEXnet Connect with Your Product

InstallAnywhere 8.0

Your application lifecycle should not end when your customer installs your application. As a software vendor, the success of your application goes well beyond the initial installation on the end user's desktop. Customers expect to have easy access to product updates, enhancements, and critical information. Your ability to communicate with your customers and monitor the health of your application is vital to your ongoing growth and profitability.

FLEXnet Connect allows you to initiate notifications to your customers. Use the following sections to integrate the FLEXnet Connect application with yours to ensure your customers receive updates and other critical information for your product:

  • Adding an Enable Update Notifications Action
  • Customizing an Enable Update Notifications Action
  • Removing an Enable Update Notifications Action
