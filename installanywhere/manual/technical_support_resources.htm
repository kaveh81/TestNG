Macrovision's Product Support Services team offers a set of comprehensive resources to help you find the answers you need. Comprehensive user documentation for your Macrovision product can be found in the product help library. Also, online resources such as our Knowledge Base provide quick access to years of experience at no cost, while maintenance plans give you more robust support with our expert engineers. Take a look at your options below and see which solution is right for you.

Knowledge Base

The Knowledge Base is accessible from the Support section of the Macrovision Web site at http://www.macrovision.com/support/index.shtml. It contains answers to many commonly asked questions and includes new information about InstallAnywhere that may not appear in the documentation. Click InstallAnywhere on this page to open the InstallAnywhere-specific knowledge base. You can use the Knowledge Base search engine to search articles by phrases, numbers, platforms, and version.

Support Site

In addition to enabling you to search the Knowledge Base, the Macrovision Support site at http://www.macrovision.com/support/index.shtml helps you:

  • Obtain InstallAnywhere updates.
  • Submit InstallAnywhere feedback.
  • View webinars.
  • Perform InstallAnywhere registration.
  • Obtain InstallAnywhere white papers, case studies, marketing materials, and training materials.
  • Obtain object libraries and sample projects.

Online Communities

The online communities are another excellent resource when you have questions about using InstallAnywhere. In these communities, users like you share tips and ideas and help each other get the most out of InstallAnywhere. Visit the communities in the Support section of our Web site, available at http://community.macrovision.com.
